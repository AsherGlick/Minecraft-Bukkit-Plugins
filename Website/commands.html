Regions Commands

Claim

/claim <region name>
Use this command to claim a 8x8 plot of land with a given name for $5000. The corners are marked with four torches.

Expand

/expand
Add another a 8x8 plot of land to an adjacent plot that you own for $1000. The corners are marked with four torches.

Dark Expand

/dark-expand
/darkexpand
/dx
Add another a 8x8 plot of land to an adjacent plot that you own for $1000. No torches are placed. Useful for claiming plots that you have already built in an dont need torches for.

Rename

/rename <new name>
Changes the name of the plot you are standing in for $6000.

Add Owner

/add-owner <player name>
/addowner <player name>
/ao <player name>
Add another player as an owner of your plot. Owners have full rights and can act as if they claimed the plot in the first place. Owners cannot be removed from plots.

List Owners

/list-owners [plot name]
/owners [plot name]
Lists the owners of a given plot. If no plot is given then it lists the owners of the plot that you are currently standing in.

Add builder

/add-builder <player name>
/addbuilder <player name>
/ab <player name>
Add a player as a builder to your plot, they can break and place blocks while they are a builder but they cannot expand your plot or add other builders. Builders can be added or removed at any time by an owner.

Remove builder

/remove-builder <player name>
/removeowner <player name>
/rb <player name>
Removes a player as a builder from your plot.

List builders

/list-builders [plot name]
/listbuilders [plot name]
/builders [plot name]
Lists all of the players who are currently assigned as builders to the plot specified. If no plot is given then it lists the builders of the plot you are currently standing in.

Economy Commands

Money

/money
Shows you how much money you have in your bank

Shop

/shop
Teleports you to the shop from your location or from the shop back to the location you teleported from.

Price

/price
Shows you how much the item you are currently holding costs and how much it would sell to the shop for.

Grant

/grant <player> <money>
Gives an ammount of your money to a specified player. Good for trading or helping out new players.
